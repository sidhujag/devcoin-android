Have your Devcoins always with you, in your pocket! You pay by quickly scanning a QR-code. As a merchant, you receive payments reliably and instantly. Devcoin Wallet is the first mobile Devcoin app, and arguably also the most secure!

FEATURES:

• No registration, web service or cloud needed! This wallet is de-centralized and peer to peer.
• Display of Devcoin amount in DVC and mDVC.
• Conversion to and from national currencies.
• Sending and receiving of Devcoin via NFC, QR-codes or Devcoin URLs.
• Address book for regularly used Devcoin addresses.
• When you're offline, you can still pay via Bluetooth.
• System notification for received coins.
• App widget for Devcoin balance.

You can find more about Devcoin at
http://devcoin.org

If you want to contribute to Devcoin Wallet, the project is located at
